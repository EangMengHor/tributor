Dale Carnegie

1892-1955

One of the greatest influencer of the 20th century

Dale Carnegie was an American writer and lecturer, and the developer of courses in self-improvement, salesmanship, coportate training, public speaking, and interpersonal skills. Born into poverty on a farm in Missouri, he was the author of How to Win Friends and Influence People, a bestseller that remains popular today. He also wrote How to Stop Worrying and Start Living, and several other books.
